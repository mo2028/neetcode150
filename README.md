## How to Study

1. **Attempt first** — Spend 15-20 minutes trying the problem on your own before looking at hints.
2. **If stuck after 20 min** — Study the solution, understand *why* it works, then close it and re-implement from scratch.
3. **Note the pattern, not the answer** — Focus on the underlying technique (sliding window, two pointers, topological sort, etc.), not memorizing code.
4. **Spaced repetition** — Revisit problems you struggled with after 3-7 days. Use the notes template (`TEMPLATE.md`) to track revisit dates.
5. **Pace** — 2-3 problems per day is sustainable (~7-10 weeks for all 150).
